Adding new Soldiers

Manual entry

Add new Soldiers by selecting the Soldiers Tab on the main navigation bar at the top of your screen.

Your current Soldiers populate the list on the left side of the screen. Click the Add Soldier button to launch the New Soldier Wizard.

Required fields are marked with asterisks ( * ). You may optionally attach a photograph of the Soldier.

Import

Soldiers can also be imported into your database from .cqpx files that have been generated by any copy of Counselor. See "Importing data" for additional details.
